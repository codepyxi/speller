Answer-0) Pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis [...] is an English word that refers to a lung disease that is otherwise known as silicosis. 
It is the longest word in the English language published in a dictionary, the Oxford English Dictionary, which 
defines it as "an artificial long word said to mean a lung disease caused by inhaling very fine ash and 
sand dust."
